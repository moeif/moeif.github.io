About Meowsay Games

Meowsay Games (猫语星球游研社) is a personal independent studio in Shanghai, China. With a passion for text-based immersive gaming experiences, our talented team of developers, designers and artists are dedicated to bringing innovative and high-quality word games to the world of player art. We pride ourselves on excellence, creativity and our commitment to pushing the boundaries of what's possible in the games industry.

Meowsay Games（猫语星球游研社）是在中国上海的个人独立工作室。 怀着对文字沉浸式游戏体验的热情，我们才华横溢的开发人员、设计师和艺术家团队致力于为玩家艺术世界带来创新和高品质的文字游戏。 我们对卓越、创造力和突破游戏行业可能的界限的承诺感到自豪。
